◆ PositSpec

enum posit::PositSpec

Specifies how a family of posit numbers behaves with respect to special numbers The fundamental special number is the opposite of zero in the posit wheel.

Signless infinity has been removed

Enumerator
WithNan 

never consider special numbers. The top value is never present

WithNanInfs 

one top Nan (or Nar using posit spec terminology)

WithInfs 

one top Nan and two signed Infinites (for more float compatibility)

one top Nan and two signed Infinites (for more float compatibility)
